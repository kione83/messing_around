Eric Church

Eric Church is a multi-instrumentalist, composer, and arranger. He is the founder of the Eric Church Music Group, which produces and arranges music for the church and community. He is also the author of the book, "The Music of Eric Church" (2014).

Rolling Stones

Rolling Stones is an American rock band formed in London in 1962. The band consists of: Mick Jagger, Keith Richards, Brian Jones, Charlie Watts, and Bill Wyman. The band's musical style is derived from the blues rock genre, and incorporates elements of hard rock, psychedelic rock, and psychedelic pop.

Coldplay

Coldplay is an English rock band formed in 1996 in Cambridge, England. The band consists of lead vocalist and keyboardist Chris Martin, lead guitarist Jonny Buckland, bassist and backing vocalist Guy Berryman, and drummer Will Champion. The band's music is characterized by a blend of alternative rock, pop rock, and progressive rock.

Shawn Mendes

Shawn Mendes is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer. He is known for his work as a lead singer in the American rock band Panic! At Home, and for his work as a member of the band's first line-up, including the singles "I Don't Wanna Live Forever" and "I Don't Wanna Live Forever (Remix)".
